For an annual PDF subscription emailed to you, email Sue and consider donating $5-­10 to our Lesbian Emergency Fund for one year's PDF subscription.
Email Sue and Avril to discuss writing for The L­-Word by the middle of the month if you're not a regular contributor.

Articles are generally 350-­700 words and our layout deadline is generally the last Sunday of each month.

Select poems are published four times a year to celebrate the equinoxes and solstices. You can submit poetry year-­round.

Topics should relate to our lesbian experiences (particularly on the north coast!) and things that affect us.
